GM Kit
As an avid D&D player and GM, I like to bring my passion of apps and D&D together to make things that help me run my games. After hooking up a projector to project maps down onto the table that I use when I run my games, I decided to make an app to help with displaying maps.
The app itself just takes in any image and allows users to create walls on the image that will be used for the dynamic lighting. Over time other small useful features have been added, like local audio track management, random name/info generators, and limited smart bulb support.
GM Kit can be found here: GM Kit
